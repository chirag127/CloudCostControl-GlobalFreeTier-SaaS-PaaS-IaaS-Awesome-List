**Goal:** To maintain the most accurate and comprehensive catalog of globally accessible, genuinely free-tier services, categorized by IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS models. This list prioritizes long-term viability and developer utility over temporary promotions.

**Scope Inclusion Criteria:**
*   **Perpetual Free Tier:** Offers must be a standing, published free tier, not a limited-time trial or promotional credit.
*   **High Utility:** Services must provide significant value to modern DevOps, MLOps, or Web/Mobile development workflows.
*   **Global Availability:** Must be accessible to users in major geographic regions (exceptions noted).
